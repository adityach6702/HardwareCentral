What is Hardware Central?

Hardware central is a site designed to help people learn more about the various key hardware components used in almost every project. This site covers the fundamentals of components such as Raspberry Pi, Arduino boards and multiple important sensors. Hardware Central also gives you the option to make your project before hand by simply dragging and dropping components and seeing how they function.
